EasyRecruit is the biggest recruiting company in Romania. It was founded in Sibiu almost 10 years ago
by a group of 5 ambitious students, who believed they can offer what is best on the market when it comes
to quick and effortless recruiting services. After almost a decade, the company has more than 250
employees in 3 locations based in Sibiu, Munchen and London. Our goal is to provide the absolute best
services on the market while reaching out to clients around Europe and beyond.
